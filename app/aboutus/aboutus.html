About Us

At Deedit.org we believe anyone can assist charities to raise funds.
So we've developed this cool platform where you can interact with video ads of brands, and in return they will donate money to a charity of your choise.

In other words, every ad you watch will generate a donation!

With Deedit, anyone can be a philanthropist and become a Deeder from anywhere at any time.
It’s Simple, Free, Fun, and above all – IMPORTANT!

The coins

The golden coins symbolize the money donation that you raise.
All your coins will be collected with other users (Deeders) coins, and then we'll convert them to real money that will be given to the charities you supported.
Each charity has a status bar where we'll publish the amount of real money they've collected.
